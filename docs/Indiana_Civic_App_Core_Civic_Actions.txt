
### Core Civic Actions for Indiana Civic Engagement App (Gen Z & Millennials)

Phase labels: [P1] = MVP (Phase 1), [P2] = Phase 2, [P3] = Phase 3
XP values are canonical — see HoosierCiv_XP_Badge_System.txt for full reference.

---

1. Legislative Engagement

- [P1] Call a Legislator
  Pick from pre-written talking points about a local bill.
  XP: +20 | Difficulty: Hard | Completion: self_report + call log

- [P2] Email a Legislator
  Quick "one-click" templates sent directly to the legislator's office.
  XP: +15 | Difficulty: Medium | Completion: self_report

- [P2] Social Share
  Share a pre-written civic message about a bill or event via the native share sheet
  (share_plus Flutter package). No specific platform required.
  XP: +10 | Difficulty: Easy | Completion: self_report

- [P1] Bill Quiz
  Short micro-lesson about a current Indiana bill, auto-generated by Claude API.
  Earn XP for answering correctly. No human review required.
  XP: +15 | Difficulty: Medium | Completion: quiz

---

2. Voting & Civic Participation

- [P1] Check Voter Registration
  Confirm voter registration status or update info via Indiana state portal.
  XP: +10 | Difficulty: Easy | Completion: api_verified

- [P2] Set Voting Reminders
  Add election day or absentee ballot deadline reminders to device calendar.
  XP: +5 | Difficulty: Easy | Completion: self_report

- [P1] Mini Voter Challenge
  Snap a photo of your "I Voted" sticker. On-device ML (tflite_flutter) confirms it's a
  sticker; EXIF date check confirms photo was taken on election day. Earns the
  "Voter Verified" badge.
  XP: +25 | Difficulty: Hard | Completion: photo_upload

---

3. Community

- [P2] Attend Town Hall
  GPS-based check-in or RSVP to local city council or district meetings.
  XP: +25 | Difficulty: Hard | Completion: gps_checkin

- [P3] Volunteer Task
  Local volunteer opportunities (1–2 hours) with manual check-in confirmation.
  XP: +30 | Difficulty: Hard | Completion: self_report

- [P3] Neighborhood Challenge
  Micro-quests like "meet one neighbor running for local office" or
  "attend a community cleanup."
  XP: +25 | Difficulty: Hard | Completion: self_report

---

4. Educational & Awareness

- [P2] Micro-Lessons
  Short interactive lessons about Indiana government, districts, or laws.
  XP: +10 | Difficulty: Easy | Completion: quiz

- [P2] Matching Game
  Match a lawmaker to their district, committee, or bill.
  XP: +10 | Difficulty: Easy | Completion: quiz

- [P3] Fact Check
  Quick quiz about local policies or civic myths.
  XP: +15 | Difficulty: Medium | Completion: quiz

---

5. Gamification Tie-ins

- Every mission earns XP points toward the next level (100 XP per level, max level 20).
- Complete a set of missions → level up with a cosmetic reward.
- Earn badges for milestones: "First Call," "Voter Verified," "3-Day Streak,"
  "Informed Advocate," "Civic Rookie" through "Civic Champ."
- Streaks: Any 1 completed mission per calendar day (local device timezone).
  Streak resets at midnight.
- Mascot Fun: Indy the Cardinal gives tips, celebrates wins, and guides missions.
